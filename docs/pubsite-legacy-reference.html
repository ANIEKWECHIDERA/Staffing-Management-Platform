For Employers

A calmer way to hire vetted staff without losing control of the process.

SkillBridge is not a public marketplace. It is a guided service operation backed by a structured internal system. Employers submit clear needs, and the team turns them into properly reviewed matches.

Structured requests

Capture role, location, arrangement, salary, start date, and special requirements clearly.

Verification-led trust

Workers are reviewed through identity, document, reference, and operational verification steps.

Explainable matching

Shortlists are shaped by fit, availability, verification status, and business rules.

Placement continuity

Placement dates, status, and guarantee periods stay visible inside the operating system.

Employer journey

High-trust flow
1. Submit request
Tell us the role, location, schedule, salary expectations, and special household needs.
2. Review shortlist
The operations team reviews fit, verification status, and availability before presenting matches.
3. Confirm placement
Once a candidate is chosen, the placement is tracked clearly with dates, status, and next steps.
